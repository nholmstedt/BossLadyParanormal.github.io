Boss Lady Paranormal

Close your eyes. Tonight is going so great you think back to the anticipation and excitement getting ready for tonight. With lingering butterflies you can't help but smile thinking he really is the perfect guy. Then suddenly it takes a turn for the worst. The last thing you remember is trying to calm down before...

Lost and confused, you find yourself wondering what happened as you realize that you are staring at your lifeless body. You've come so far in life, built a successful career, wonderful friends and family. Then suddenly it's all ripped away, almost feeling like it was all just a dream.

Haven't you wondered what happens to your soul if you are taken before your time? Wouldn’t you be sad, confused, upset, and angry towards who or what took that time from you? Wouldn't you try and reach out for help? Or to get attention in any way possible?

There are countless lost souls living amongst us who haven’t had the chance and proper guidance to find out what had happened to them, to find their peace, and to move on. Those lost souls call to us. We dig into the history and uncover the past, welcoming the tormented, the lost, the confused, and the angry. We open doors for them to communicate, and allow them to pass on. We are always learning, evolving, to align and open ourselves to these souls, just like you and I, to give them the voice they no longer have.

From different backgrounds, we ladies own our lifestyles and opportunities making us Bosses of our lives, as well as giving souls the power to be a Boss of their own soul once again. We are Boss Lady Paranormal!
